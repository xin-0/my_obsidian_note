##### basic
$z=a+bi=\alpha e^{i \theta}$
- $\alpha$: magnitude
- $\theta$: rotation angle from $x$ axis
- $\theta=\arctan(\frac{b}{a})$

$e^{i \theta}=\cos\theta+i\sin\theta$

##### conjugate
$\bar{z} =a-bi=\alpha e^{-i \theta}$

##### Properties
- $z\bar{z}=|z|^2$
- $|z||w|=|zw|$
- $z^{-1}=\frac{1}{z}=\frac{\bar{z}}{|z|^2}\quad$
